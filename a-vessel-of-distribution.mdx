## The Nature of Give & Recieve

Something that really bothers me with my work is the nature of it. Ironic since I work directly with it all the time.

I guess you can say in precise terms is the power that I am able to construct a vessel for. My work, without platitudes, is to create vessels that harness vast amount of energies honed into a blade, or in this case, a website of commerce. I create state of the art websites that are engineered to distribute the wielder's products into the market that align with their energy and hone it to get what they want out of it. It is more akin to a pot holder than a weapon of war.

As much as I personally strive for greatness in my own work, I must understand that this work team based if I am to work with a 'client'. I am even hesitant on using the word client here. As the process if more of a craftsman working with another craftsman. 

Paradoxically while my ambitions are great, they are often times smothered by the knowing that aggression in web development is often times to my detriment. Consistency in energy matters more and if you want to distribute an energy that is actually received, being aggressive and oppressive often will not work for as long as the website stands. My ambition is often honed with the understanding that abundance & growth will not arise through hate or malice, but through love and hope for a better tomorrow. 

In retrospect, it sounds somewhat utterly disappointing. In many of top companies often times they are ruled by psychopaths and villains. Scum of the earth. However, in a twisted way, they must love and hope that what they do will bring their better tomorrow to them as these passions and ambitions they have are utterly selfish. And so are mine. I am not different than they are except in the way I go about about gaining it and what exactly I am after. What I am after is private. But never make mistake that you can't ever see what it is before it is gained. As there are always patterns of morality and philosophy in every action done by any man or woman.

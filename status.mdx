_A replacement for the Sanity Connect App for Shopify_

\_Aggregate Connect is a Shopify app that connects your Shopify storefront data to Sanity.

### Why \_Aggregate Connect?

The Sanity Connect App isn't updated often and lacks critical support due to its lack of adoption. This means that any major updates in Shopify's API creates downtime for any new build we might have. 

In our case, Sanity is taking roughly a month to update its data collection from Shopify regarding the major Collections API update. Sanity's lack of dependable updates isn't healthy and shouldn't be relied upon.

\_Aggregate Connect stays up to date with major Shopify API changes and replaces Sanity's Shopify Connect App.

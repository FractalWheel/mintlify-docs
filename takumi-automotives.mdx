## What Is Takumi?

Takumi is a demo e-commerce store meant to show high-end web development using traditional web dev techniques mixed with the modern abstract and innovative new ways of viewing the web.

This store includes fashion, accessories, and supplements. 

> Definition of name - Takumi: "Takumi carries one of the most respected vocabulary meanings in Japanese — _takumi_ (匠) means "master craftsman" or "artisan," embodying the cultural ideal of devoted skill and craft. The single-kanji 匠 writing is direct and clean. Takumi is one of the breakout boys' names of the 2000s and 2010s."
